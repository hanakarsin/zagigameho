It is back to school, and Kenyan parents have been busy getting their children ready.

On Wednesday, July 28, 2021, celebrated Mugithi singer Samidoh shared a lovely photo with his daughter in uniform.

In the image, father and daughter stood with their hands crossed while seemingly having a heart to heart.

The young girl wore a grey skirt, white shirt, red tie and blue sweater as she stared at her father.

In the caption, Samidoh opined that the best thing a parent could offer their children is mentorship.
