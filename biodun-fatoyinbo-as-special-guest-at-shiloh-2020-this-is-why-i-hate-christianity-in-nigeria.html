'Biodun Fatoyinbo As Special Guest At Shiloh 2020?' - This Is Why I Hate Christianity In Nigeria

June 2024 · 2 minute read

A Nigerian lady identified as ‘@Hermosa_Reinaa’ on Twitter, has condemned the invitation of Pastor Biodun Fatoyinbo of the Commonwealth of Zion Assembly, COZA as a special guest at Shiloh 2020.

The lady called out Pastor David Oyedepo, the founder and presiding Bishop Living Faith Church Worldwide, also known as Winners’ Chapel International for bringing on Fatoyinbo to his yearly programme despite the clergy been accused of rape.

Naija News recalls that popular Nigerian singer, Timi Dakolo in 2019 accused Biodun Fatoyinbo of sleeping with his female church members, including his wife, Busola Dakolo.

The revelation which made the top headlines in the media then has, however, been swept under the rug after Justice Oathman Musa of the Federal High Court in Abuja held that the case against Fatoyinbo was empty and purely sentimental and awarded costs of N1m against Busola Dakolo.

In the same vein then, the Christian Association of Nigeria (CAN) admonish the celebrity photographer, Busola Dakolo to put the past behind her and reconcile with Pastor Fatoyinbo.

However, the invitation of the embattled clergy to Shiloh 2020 erupted series of reactions on the micro-blogging site after @Hermosa_Reinaa took to her account to note that such cases are why she wants nothing to do with Christianity in Nigeria.

Her tweet reads: “Biodun Fatoyinbo special guest at Shiloh. This is one of the reasons why I want nothing with Christianity in Nigeria.”
