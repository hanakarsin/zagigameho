15+ saddest songs about losing a friend who passed away

August 2024 · 8 minute read

Death is inevitable. When a friend passes on, it is natural to feel lost, heartbroken, and depressed. Music has always been one way of mourning departed loved ones. Songs about losing a friend are expressive and depressing. Musicians sing about how they miss those that have died, meeting the departed once again, and celebrating their life on earth.

There is no formula to grieve the loss of a friend. Some people go quiet while other talks about the friend. Others choose to mourn in solitude, while other people want to be with friends and family when in grief. Being a musician, one gets to express self when going through different emotions. Talking about grief is one way artists express themselves through their art.

Songs about losing a friend

It can be so challenging to lose a friend. It's easy to feel all alone, mainly because that friend might have been the exact person you'd turn to when you felt sad. Here are the top 20 songs about losing a friend that will help you overcome the grief.
