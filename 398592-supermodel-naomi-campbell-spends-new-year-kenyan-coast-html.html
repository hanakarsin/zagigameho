The stunning snaps included those of the beach, the beautiful sun and the ocean of the location where she spent the better part of her festive season.

Her captions were all about praising the "beautiful Kenya" with the hashtag #NaomiAfrica to indicate she was working on an African-based project.

Speaking of projects, the supermodel recently held an interview with online sensation Elsa Majimbo in a show titled No Filter With Naomi Naomi.

The commediane also noted there was a lot of colourism in the country which denied her several opportunities as she was dark-skinned.

This part of the video went viral on social media with many sharing their thoughts on her sentiments.

Many seemed to concur with her saying it was nothing new as it happened in several places.
