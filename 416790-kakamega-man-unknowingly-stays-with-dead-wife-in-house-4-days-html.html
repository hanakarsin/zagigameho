Residents of Lurambi constituency, Kakamega county, on Friday, June 25, woke up to a shocking incident after discovering the body of a decomposing of a middle-aged woman in a rental house four days after she went missing.

The deceased was reportedly living with her alleged boyfriend, who, according to residents, unknowingly stayed with the corpse due to 'alcoholic influence'.

According to a report by NTV Kenya, the body of Onyancha Atieno was discovered after a pungent smell emanated from Issa Musindalu's house.

The incident was confirmed by the area's Nyumba Kumi chairman, who attributed the woman's death to the consumption of excessive illicit brew.

"The deceased's body had already decomposed. I urge residents to avoid taking illicit brews as there are many licensed joints where they can buy alcohol," he said.

Body of Nyeri man buried secretly by his lover exhumed

TUKO.co.ke understands the late Hudson Wanjohi Ndumia passed away on May 10 and was buried on May 22 by his secret lover who lived in Kagongo, Tetu constituency.

The woman who is in her 50s was accused of presenting a burial permit while collecting Ndumia’s remains from the Mathari Consolata Mission Hospital morgue in Nyeri town, contrary to the wishes of his family.

According to reliable sources, Ndumia, who hails from Makuyu in Murang’a county, lived with his secret lover, a widow, in Tetu, Nyeri county.

Ndumia had allegedly fallen out with his wife, prompting the relocation.
