February 2024 · 10 minute read

Good morning Nigeria and welcome to October 1, Nigeria’s Independent Day for the year 2022.

A happy Independence Day to you, your friends and your family from Naija News Team.

Naija News reports that Nigeria celebrates Independence Day every first day of October. Today being October 1st, Nigeria turns 62 years old. The West African country got its Independence from Britain.
