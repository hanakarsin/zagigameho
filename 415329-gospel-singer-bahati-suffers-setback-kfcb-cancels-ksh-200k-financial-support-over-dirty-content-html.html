Gospel Singer Bahati Suffers Setback as KFCB Cancels KSh 200k Financial Support over Dirty Content

June 2024 · 3 minute read
  • The KFCB CEO Ezekiel Mutua has announced the board has cancelled a KSh 200,000 and other kinds of support to gospel singer Bahati
  • Mutua said the decision followed the posting of videos that contravened clean content and partnership policy
  • The KFCB boss further revealed Bahati had approached the board for support and in return, he was to commit to being a clean content ambassador

The Kenya Film Classification Board (KFCB) has cancelled over KSh 200,000 and other support to gospel artiste Bahati.

The KFCB CEO Ezekiel Mutua made the announcement on Saturday, June 12, saying the decision followed the posting of videos that contravened clean content and partnership policy.

In a statement posted on his social media pages, Mutua claimed the decision was a painful one because Bahati approached the board for support for his album's launch.

In return, the gospel singer was expected to commit to being a clean content ambassador.

"However, with the videos doing rounds on social media, our team unanimously took the decision to cancel the support and disassociate completely with the planned event," wrote Mutua.

My heart breaks at seeing our artists going to extremes to promote explicit content even at the expense of tainting their brand and denting their careers," he added.

From a professional perspective, clean content, family suitable content is more marketable and sustainable than dirty and putrid content, argued the KFCB boss.

Bahati smoking cigar

Bahati caused a stir on social media on June 7, 2021, after posting a video of himself smoking a cigar.

TUKO.co.ke reported the video depicted Bahati wearing a suit, sunglasses and a fedora while puffing away at the cigar as a woman stood by his side.

From the way he puffed and billowed the smoke, it was evident that he was no novice.

Given that he is still perceived as a gospel musician, the smoking video swiftly attracted backlash online.
