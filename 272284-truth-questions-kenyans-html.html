August 2024 · 9 minute read

What is so serious about Truth or Dare; nothing really. It is but a verbal game for parties where two or more people participate in a round of questions and answers. Of course, it is more than just answering a few random questions put in front of you. It is one of the most fun-filled party games you could try out with a group of friends. What makes it awesome is that you are not allowed to back down from the path you have opted for, once you have declared your choice. I thought of making things cooler for you by suggesting a crazy collection of truth or dare questions. But remember; try it at your own risk. Once you choose “truth”, you will have answer honestly to the question asked. If you choose “dare”, you are going to have to do what has been asked of you; but that cannot be decided without being in the situation. For now, try out the list of best truth or dare questions.
