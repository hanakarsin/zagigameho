How to unlock Spectranet MiFi

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Do you want to learn how to unlock Spectranet MiFi modem so that you can use any sim card in it? Follow the steps explained below carefully to unlock your modem successfully.

Just like most modems in Nigeria, Spectranet MiFi Modem is a Huawei e5573 MiFi device which is used by most networks in Nigeria to offer the lightning-fast 4G network to their customers. Some of the commonly used Huawei modem devices used in Nigeria are the MTN e5573 320 and the Spectranet e5573 606 variant. It is possible to use some other network sim card on these devices after unlocking them except of Glo sim in the MTN e5573 320 variant. Unlocking your Spectranet MiFi modem will open you to the opportunity of using other unsupported sim cards in it. You will need to download a modem unlocking software and some other drivers to do this. Learn the steps below.

Step 1

  • Unzip and install the drivers on your computer.
  • Download the latest Huawei Hilink drivers from this link: https://www.datafilehost.com/d/d35c3c52.
  • Download the Huawei Hilink driver V5 from this link: https://www.datafilehost.com/d/a7f56ed9.
  • Unzip the drivers and install them on your computer.
  • After the installation is complete, restart your computer so that the drivers can be fully loaded before you proceed to the next steps.
