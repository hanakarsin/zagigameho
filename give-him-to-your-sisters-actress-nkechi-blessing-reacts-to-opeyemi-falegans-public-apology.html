Actress Nkechi Blessing Reacts To Opeyemi Falegan's Public Apology

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Controversial Nollywood actress Nkechi Blessing has reacted to the unreserved public apology of her ex-lover and Ekiti state politician Opeyemi Falegan.

Naija News earlier reported that Opeyemi Falegan in an interview with media personality, Daddy Freeze apologised to Nkechi Blessing for the pains inflicted on her following their messy breakup.

Barely an hour after the video surfaced online, Nkechi Blessing took to her Instagram page to share a jaw-dropping photo of herself as she gushes over her beauty.

Captioning the post, Nkechi Blessing who seemed to have sighted the video of Opeyemi Falegan’s public apology online dropped a cryptic post.

Nkechi Blessing said ladies should learn to evolve and make sure their ex-lovers don’t meet them at the same position where they left them.

nkechi_blessing_sunday wrote: “Just make sure they don’t meet you where they left you Evolve baby girl, The Billionaire”.

Reacting to the post, a female fan complimented Nkechi Blessing’s beauty pleading with her to forgive Opeyemi Falegan and accept him back.

The fan added that Nkechi Blessing is a strong woman and should forgive her ex-lover for everything he had made her pass through.

Nkechi Blessing seemed displeased with the comment as she urged the fan to give her sisters to Opeyemi Falegan.

nkechi_blessing_sunday wrote: @esther.ndaka “go and give him to your sisters,no let God p#nish you ma”
