Ali Kiba education

Ali Kiba lliked to play around with music all the time. He completed his primary schooling at the Upanga Primary School located in Dar es Salaam and then moved on to high school in order to pursue secondary education.

Alikiba music career

Ali Kiba was fond of music and dance during his childhood. He used to sing and dance along with his friends and also take part in drama singing shows while he was in school. Soon after his 12th exam (2004), he took up the career of a musician and a songwriter and quickly went on to compose his career’s first song named “Maria” which is highly applaudable. He also began to work on his career’s first album at the later stages of the year. In 2005, he refused the offer to play soccer in the Ugandan team in order to develop his career in the music industry. He decided to give more attention to singing and composing rather than playing football.

Let us now look into the history of his charismatic music career till date:

  • 2007 – In this year, Ali Kiba launched his 1st album named “Cinderella”, which brought him into the limelight for building an astonishing career which he used to dream of since his childhood. The album was a big hit in the entire market. The single song, Cinderella, was really amazing and surprisingly topped the music charts in Tanzania. The song was also liked by the people in the neighboring countries of Kenya, Uganda and Rwanda. The entire East Africa started humming his songs and even dancing to its tune soon after he released more scintillating songs like Nakshi Mrembo, Mac Muga, Ndugu Yangu and many more.
  • 2008 – In 2008, he came up with a singles album named “AliKiba 4 Real”. A single from the album – “Usiniseme” was a massive hit of the year. It dominated the East African music arena at that point in time.
  • 2009 – Ali Kiba tried his luck in modeling and was quite successful in it. The singing star also kept himself connected with music. He again, as predicted by all, refused to be part of a soccer team named African Lyon from Tanzania.
  • 2010 – He was able to crack a mega deal with One8 which offered him a chance to be able to share the stage with the international sensation R Kelly. He also got to work with 7 other singing superstars of Africa. Soon, he was allowed to perform on singles rather in duet as R Kelly was highly impressed by the singer's beautiful & unique voice. This opportunity provided him with the wings to fly high and spread his music all around the world.
  • 2011 – The experience of working with the star, R Kelly, helped him a lot. Ali Kiba soon released a single – “Dushelele” which he had both co-directed and co-produced. After the latest success of the single, he released another blockbuster song named “Single Boy/Single Girl” featuring the mesmerizing Tanzanian star Lady Jaydee ,which goes on to annihilate the competition in this genre. He was selected as “the most popular top selling artist in East Africa” given by the Synovate Research.
  • 2012 – In the year 2012, he tried his luck in acting by taking acting lessons from the experts. He even tried to write some movie scripts.
  • 2013 & 2014 – He started working for his next album. He was also busy writing scripts for his 1st movie.
