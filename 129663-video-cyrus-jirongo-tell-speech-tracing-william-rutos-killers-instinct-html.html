Cyrus Jirongo accuse William Ruto of having a killer's instinct

March 2024 · 2 minute read

- Former Lugari MP Cyrus Jirongo has sensationally claimed that William Ruto has a 'killer's instinct' having once killed someone in early 2000s

- Jirongo went ahead to liken Ruto and Jacob Juma's relationship to that of the biblical Abel and Cain in which Cain killed his brother out of jealous

- He further dared William Ruto to take legal action against him as he is willing to prove he is behind the murder of Jacob Juma

Cyrus Jirongo, a former close ally of William Ruto, has pressed on with his claims that the deputy president murdered controversial businessman Jacob Juma.

He said that Ruto was at the center of the murder of a man at a rally in Trans Nzoia then.
