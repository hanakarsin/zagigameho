NCDC Confirms 220 New Cases Of Coronavirus In Nigeria

June 2024 · 1 minute read

The Nigerian Centre for Disease Control (NCDC) on Saturday at 11:50 pm confirmed 220 new cases of coronavirus in Nigeria.

This brings the total number of cases in the country to 2388.

According to the breakdown by NCDC, 62 new cases were recorded in Lagos, while, 52 in FCT, 31 in Kaduna, 13 in Sokoto, 10 in Kebbi, 9 in Yobe, 6 in Borno, 5 in Edo, 5 in Bauchi, 4 in Gombe, 4 in Enugu, 4 in Oyo, 3 in Zamfara, 2 in Nasarawa, 2 in Osun, 2 in Ebonyi, 2 in Kwara, 2 in Kano and 2 in Plateau.
