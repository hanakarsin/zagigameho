Nadia Buari Gives Birth To Twins?

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Beautiful Ghanaian actress, Nadia Buari has reportedly given birth to twins.

According to a Ghanaian news website, Myjoyonline, her father, Sidiku Buari confirmed the good news saying: “It’s true. She brought forth twins alright, that is true. I’m confirming [it] as the father.”

Her father is reported to have revealed that his daughter gave birth to a set of twin girls in a hospital outside Ghana.

Details regarding the father of the twins, location of birth or where the actress is presently are yet unknown.

There have been a lot of congratulatory messages on Twitter but so far Nadia Buari herself has neither confirmed nor denied.

The actress, who has been absent from the entertainment scene for some time now has been linked to Nollywood actor Jim Iyke.

Their relationship which they have been flaunting on social media has been trailed with a lot of controversies. They have got many of their fans confused on whether they are a couple or not. In March 2014, she had gotten engaged to the actor in one of the episodes of his reality TV show, Jim Iyke Unscripted.

Not long afterwards, report had gone viral that Nadia Buari dumped him.

Despite their on/off relationship, Nadia Buari had taken Instagram to wish him a happy birthday when he clocked 38 in September 2014.
