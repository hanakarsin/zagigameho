SBM Bank Kenya profile

SBM Bank Kenya Limited is a subsidiary of SBM Bank founded in 1973 and has its headquarters in Mauritius. It officially opened in doors in Kenya in May 2017 after acquiring Fidelity Bank. The entry of SBM Holding in Kenya brings extensive financial hub expertise in handling investments.

This Kenyan bank works with a vision to be a leading financial service provider. It also operates with a mission to use its exceptional services and unique product to create lasting relationships with customers. That way, the bank can bring the expected returns to its shareholders and still support community development.

So what does SBM stand for? SBM Bank in full is State Bank of Mauritius. The SBM Kenya swift code is SBMKKENA or SBMKKENAXXX, whereby the last three Xs represent a specific branch code.

Products and services

SBM Bank Kenya Limited offers services for both personal and businesses. For instance, under the current account category, there are several options such as SBM Jivunie current account, Aspire current account, Aspire Plus current account, Elite current account, Plus current account, and Business Plus current account.

SBM Mfukoni mobile banking

This secure app is designed to allow you, as the SBM customer, to go cashless yet control your account. Some of the key benefits and features of having Mfukono mobile app include:

  • Ability to transfer funds at anytime
  • Pay bills such as electricity and internet bills.
  • Top-up airtime for free for yourself or someone else.
  • Check and download statements for free.
  • View and update your account profile
